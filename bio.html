Bio

I am a Member of Data Science Team at Acumen Intelligence. My role involves developing machine learning and AI solutions that strengthen Acumen’s data-driven products

I am currently reading for a BSc (Hons) in Computer Science, where I have contributed to data science projects such as Rewire, an application designed to help people reduce behavioral addictions. I am also conducting research on a Sinhala speech-to-text model, narrowed to healthcare applications.

Before moving into data science, I worked as a trainee full stack developer at Revoise, where I first became fascinated by the potential of data-driven solutions. That early exposure led me toward machine learning and AI, and eventually into healthcare domain.

Outside of data science, I enjoy long distance trekking, being out for days with nothing but a trail ahead. I’m also into photography, mostly capturing buildings, sunsets, mountains, and the sea. Some of my work’s on VSCO.
