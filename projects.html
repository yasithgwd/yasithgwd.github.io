Projects

  1. Healthie-AI

    Retrieval‑Augmented Generation (RAG) medical assistant that answers questions about diseases, symptoms, and preventive measures using a curated corpus of trusted health sources.

  1. Medi-Sense

    Symptom checker for disease prediction and health recommendations built with machine learning. Helps users identify potential medical conditions based on symptoms and provides personalized precautions, medications, diet plans, and exercise recommendations.

  1. EmoReads

    Emo-Reads is a semantic book recommender that matches readers with titles by mood, not just keywords. It blends vector search, zero-shot genre tagging, and LLM‑based sentiment/emotion extraction to surface books that “feel” right—whether you want cozy optimism, dark academia vibes, or cathartic heartbreak.

  1. Rewire

    mobile application that is designed to assist individuals in overcoming addictions through a structured, step by step approach without the use of medication. The app leverages an Ai-driven chatbot and questionnaires to assess addiction levels and categorize addictions into substance based or behavioral types.
