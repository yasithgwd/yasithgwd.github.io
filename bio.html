Bio

I am a Member of Data Science Team at Acumen Intelligence, a company focused on B2B lead generation. My role involves developing machine learning and AI solutions that strengthen Acumen’s data-driven products, while personally exploring their potential in healthcare applications.

I am currently reading for a BSc (Hons) in Computer Science, where I have contributed to projects such as Rewire, a mobile app designed to help people reduce behavioral addictions. I am also conducting research on a Sinhala speech-to-text model, narrowed to healthcare applications.

Before moving into data science, I worked as a Full Stack Developer at Revoise, a startup where I first became fascinated by the potential of data-driven solutions. That early exposure led me toward machine learning and AI, and eventually into healthcare-related applications.

Beyond technology, I enjoy long-distance trekking and photography. I especially love capturing architectural buildings, sunsets, mountains, and coastlines. It’s my way of slowing down and noticing detail in both the built and natural world. You can find some of my work on VSCO
